first time login:
user choose a department, then done:

returning users:
user choose what he/she will do in library (PURPOSE OF VISIT), there will be a checkbox:
"borrowing books", "computer use", "reading", "studying", "thesis work", click "Enter Session"

then the record will save. redirect to dashboard.

in dashboard, there is a table that shows the history of "PURPOSE OF VISIT", where it shows all the historical data of user everytime it visits.
in dashboard, there is also a logout button.


admin login:
automatically redirects to dashboard, having other options like user management, etc.
